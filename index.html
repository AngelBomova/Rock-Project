About Coldstone...

Hello. You already know my name by now. I am actually not who I seem. I was born in the 1980, but reborn in 2022, after I was revamped by my creator. I was born and raised in the parking lot of an orthodontic place in the middle of a plaza. I spent most of life there, with my parents and thousands of other rocks just like me. Long years, through rain, snow, and people stepping on me, I finally saw new scenery when I was picked up and put in a backpack one afternoon. I was there for what felt like forever, until I was placed on a desk, it was a classroom. Soon after, the experiments started. After long days of burning hot glue, getting a spray tan, arms, legs, eyes, and a hair transplant, I was reborn on December 20th 2022. It was hard to say goodbye to my old life, but now I have more freedom than ever, cramped with thousands of other rocks, I am treated like a KING!
